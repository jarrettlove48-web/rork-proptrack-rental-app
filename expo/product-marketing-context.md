## Product

**Category:** Mobile app (iOS)
**Website:** https://proptrack.app
**Support:** support@proptrack.app

### What it does
PropTrack is a maintenance tracking and tenant management app for small landlords who own 1–5 rental units. It replaces the chaos of managing rentals via text message with a structured, simple tool for tracking maintenance requests, managing tenants, logging expenses, and communicating — all in one place.

### Core value props
1. **Stop managing rentals through text messages** — Every request is tracked from open to resolved
2. **Tenants submit requests directly** — No more midnight texts, tenants have their own portal
3. **Expense tracking built in** — Log what you spend so tax season isn't a nightmare
4. **Simple enough to actually use** — Not built for property management companies, built for you

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Small landlords in the US who own 1–5 rental units
- Often have a full-time job, landlording is a side income
- Manage properties themselves (no property manager)
- Currently use text messages, email, or nothing to track maintenance
- Not tech-savvy — need something that just works
- Age range: 35–60
- Own a single-family rental, duplex, or small multifamily

**Secondary:** Accidental landlords — people who inherited a property or couldn't sell their old home

**Anti-audience:** Professional property managers, investors with 10+ units, property management companies (they use AppFolio, Buildium, etc.)
